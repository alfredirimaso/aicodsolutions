Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to complete a website?

Website development timelines vary based on complexity and features required. Simple business websites typically take 2-4 weeks, e-commerce sites 4-8 weeks, and complex custom applications 6-12 weeks. We provide detailed project timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the development process.

What are your pricing options for website development?

Our pricing is project-based and depends on the scope, features, and complexity. We offer three main packages: Basic (starting at UGX 500,000), Professional (starting at UGX 1,200,000), and Enterprise (custom pricing). All packages include responsive design, SEO optimization, and basic training. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages including regular updates, security patches, content updates, and technical support. Our maintenance plans start at UGX 100,000/month and include hosting, backups, and 24/7 emergency support. We also provide training for your team to manage basic content updates.

What payment terms do you offer?

We require a 40% deposit to begin work, with 30% due at the halfway point, and the final 30% upon project completion. For larger projects, we offer flexible payment plans with monthly installments. We accept bank transfers, mobile money (MTN, Airtel), and cash payments. All payments are clearly documented with receipts.

Do you work with clients outside of Kabale?

Absolutely! We serve clients throughout Uganda and internationally. We use modern collaboration tools like Zoom, Google Meet, and project management software to ensure effective communication regardless of location. For remote clients, we provide regular video updates and maintain the same high-quality service standards.

What technologies do you use for development?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (React, Vue.js), Node.js, PHP (Laravel), Python (Django), and mobile frameworks like React Native and Flutter. We choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements, ensuring scalability, security, and performance.

Can you help with existing websites or systems?

Yes, we provide comprehensive services for existing websites including maintenance, updates, redesigns, and platform migrations. We can audit your current system, identify improvements, and implement enhancements. We also offer emergency repair services and can help modernize outdated websites to current standards.

What ICT training programs do you offer?

We offer comprehensive ICT training programs including basic computer skills, Microsoft Office, web development, graphic design, digital marketing, and programming languages. Training can be conducted at our office, your premises, or online. We provide certificates upon completion and offer both individual and group training sessions.

Do you provide computer repair services?

Yes, we offer professional computer repair services including hardware repairs, software installation, virus removal, data recovery, and system optimization. We provide both on-site and in-office repair services. Our technicians are certified and we offer a 30-day warranty on all repairs. Emergency repair services are available for urgent issues.

How do you ensure website security?

We implement multiple security measures including SSL certificates, regular security updates, secure coding practices, and regular backups. We also provide security audits, malware scanning, and DDoS protection. For e-commerce sites, we ensure PCI compliance and secure payment processing. We monitor sites 24/7 for security threats.

What is your process for starting a new project?

Our process begins with a free consultation to understand your needs and goals. We then create a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing. Once approved, we start with design mockups, followed by development, testing, and deployment. We provide regular updates throughout the process and offer training before project handover.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

Yes, we provide reliable hosting services with 99.9% uptime guarantee, daily backups, and 24/7 support. We also help with domain registration, SSL certificates, and email setup. Our hosting packages start at UGX 50,000/month and include technical support. We can also help migrate existing websites to our hosting platform.
