Empowering businesses through innovative digital solutions since 2025

Who We Are

AiCOD Solutions Limited is a dynamic technology company based in Kabale, Uganda, specializing in comprehensive digital solutions for businesses and individuals. Founded in 2025, we have quickly established ourselves as a trusted partner for digital transformation in the region.

Our team combines technical expertise with deep understanding of local business needs, delivering solutions that drive real results. We believe in the power of technology to transform businesses and improve lives.
